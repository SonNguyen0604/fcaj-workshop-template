During my internship at AWS Vietnam, I have had the privilege to participate in various tech events and community tournaments. Each event offers a valuable hands-on experience that helps reinforce my technical skills, sharpen my ability to work under high pressure, and broaden my perspective on the Cloud & AI ecosystem.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)  
&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Kick-off Tech Trivia & Community Tournament  

&emsp;**Time:** 09:00 - 12:00, June 20, 2026  

&emsp;**Location:** Amazon Web Services Vietnam Office (Ho Chi Minh City) / Online  

&emsp;**Role in Event:** Tournament Participant  

&emsp;**Description:** A highly competitive tech trivia tournament featuring 8 registered teams in a single-elimination (Knockout) format designed to test rapid reflexes and foundational knowledge in Cloud, core AWS services, and Generative AI. My team competed in the 2nd match; despite an early exit, I stayed to closely observe how other teams tackled technical pitfalls until the dramatic final matchups concluded.

&emsp;**Outcomes:** Successfully identified and reviewed critical knowledge gaps (VPC, EC2, IAM, LLM parameters) through real-world quiz questions provided by mentors, while gaining valuable insights regarding role delegation and swift decision-making within a team under strict time constraints.
