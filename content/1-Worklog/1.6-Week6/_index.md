### WEEK 6 ACHIEVEMENTS: AWS DATA ANALYTICS WORKSHOP & BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

1. **Data Ingestion, Cataloging & Processing Pipeline**
   - **Ingestion & Storage:** Built efficient data ingestion pipelines to collect, store, and organize raw datasets using Amazon S3.
   - **Cataloging & Transformation:** Orchestrated metadata discovery and automated data transformation workflows using AWS Glue (Interactive Sessions, Studio, DataBrew) and Amazon EMR.

2. **Serverless Querying & Real-Time Stream Analytics**
   - **Data Warehousing:** Managed modern cloud data warehouses on Amazon Redshift and executed serverless queries using Amazon Athena.
   - **Stream Processing:** Analyzed live streaming data with Kinesis Data Analytics and structured serverless data serving via AWS Lambda functions.

3. **Enterprise Visualization & Business Intelligence**
   - **Dashboard Architecture:** Engineered and formatted comprehensive, interactive dashboards within Amazon QuickSight.
   - **Insights & Metrics:** Integrated advanced visual elements, including Line/Pie charts, Pivot tables, Data Details, and customized KPIs for precise business profiling.
