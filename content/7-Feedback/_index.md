> Here you can freely contribute personal feedback on your experiences participating in the First Cloud Journey program, helping the FCJ team improve and resolve shortcomings based on the following categories:

### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The learning and working environment at AWS Vietnam is exceptionally dynamic, professional, and deeply tech-driven. The community members and organizers are always open and ready to support students when facing infrastructure configuration difficulties. The program also introduces exciting activities like the opening Tech Trivia tournament, which effectively alleviates pressure and boosts team bonding.

**2. Mentor / Team Admin Support**  
Mentors are highly dedicated, providing clear guidance centered around the AWS Well-Architected Framework, which encourages our team to independently research and deploy the High Availability topic rather than just handing out ready-made solutions. The admin team is incredibly responsive, handling everything from sandbox provisioning to allocating the $200 Credit package smoothly to facilitate our AWS experimentation.

**3. Alignment Between Work and Academic Major**  
This Cloud Operations and infrastructure management project aligns perfectly with my Information Systems major at Saigon University (SGU). Designing custom VPC networks, administering Multi-AZ Amazon RDS engines, and crafting technical documentation allow me to digitize and practically apply system administration theories learned at university.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**  
The internship offers a profound opportunity to elevate both hard and soft skills. Technically, I have become proficient in managing code via Git/GitHub, configuring Markdown/Hugo setups, and architecting fault-tolerant cloud systems. Socially, my ability to collaborate within a team under tight timelines and translate technical documentation bilingual has significantly sharpened.

**5. Culture & Team Synergy**  
The organizational culture here highly values self-discipline, respect, and the "Fail Fast, Learn Faster" mindset. Within our 5-member team tackling a 5-week sprint, coordination has been seamless: while technical members focus strictly on provisioning labs, I proactively manage the documentation infrastructure and portfolio setup to optimize the team's velocity.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The program offers a rich self-study learning path, high-quality reference guides from the AWS Study Group community, and especially the practical Credit funding for live resources. These are immensely valuable assets for any technology student.

### Other Questions

* **What are you most satisfied with during your internship?**  
  I am most satisfied with having hands-on access to an enterprise-grade AWS Console and collaborating with an amazing group of teammates who actively share knowledge and debug system environments together.
* **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**  
  The organizing committee could integrate brief mid-week Office Hours, giving project groups more opportunities to connect deeply with Mentors for infrastructure and architecture reviews.
* **If recommending to friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?**  
  Absolutely yes. This program is the perfect launchpad for any student aspiring to pursue a professional Cloud Engineer or Cloud Operations path, thanks to its well-structured curriculum and highly practical environment.

### Suggestions & Expectations

* **What suggestions do you have to improve the internship experience?**  
  I suggest providing sample blueprint documents focusing on advanced IAM policies so that students can adopt a DevSecOps mindset much earlier in the program.
* **Do you wish to continue with this program in the future?**  
  I highly look forward to remaining active within the AWS Study Group community to support subsequent learner cohorts after concluding this internship.
* **Other Feedback (Freely share):**  
  I hope the next phase of the Tech Trivia tournament brings a dramatic redemption match for the teams that faced an early elimination in the qualifiers!
